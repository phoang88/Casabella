Welcome to Casabella Salon!

"May all who enter as GUEST, leave as FRIENDS"

We specialize in natural nail services such as manicures, pedicures, and nail enhancements. We are conveniently located in the heart of Georgetown - Washington D.C with sprawling shops, entertainment, and dining all around us.

Walk-ins are welcome; however, we advise you to call ahead or make an appointment because availability is not always guaranteed. Quality is our number one priority so grab a friend, come by, have a drink of wine, and enjoy getting pampered by our knowledgeable technicians!

Cleanliness is of the utmost importance at Casabella Salon. All instruments are sanitized and disinfected prior to use. Each client is provided with a sealed packet of disposable file, buff, and pumice stone. The salon is equipped with pipe-less, magnetic jet, pedicure chairs, and individual tube liners for each client providing the healthiest and cleanest service possible. Our guests will rest assured knowing that they are not "touching" the guest who came before them.

Call to schedule an appointment. We are here to assist you in achieving beautiful and healthy nails!
